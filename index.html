What is a travel rewards card?

Travel rewards cards turn your everyday spending into points or miles that can be used for:

  • • Flights and upgrades
  • • Hotels and vacation packages
  • • Lounge access and fast track
  • • Statement credits for travel purchases

Key benefits to look for

Points & miles

Earn extra rewards on travel categories like airlines, hotels and transport.

No FX fees

Some cards waive foreign transaction fees, making them ideal for international trips.

Lounge access

Priority Pass or issuer lounges can transform airport time into actual downtime.

Travel insurance

Trip delay, lost luggage and rental car insurance are often bundled as benefits.

How to compare travel cards

Before you apply, compare:

  • • Annual fee vs. realistic value of perks
  • • Reward earning rate on your actual spending categories
  • • FX fees if you travel abroad frequently
  • • Which airline or hotel partners you actually use

A simple travel rewards strategy

  • • Use one primary travel card for all flights and hotels
  • • Add a no-fee backup card with no FX fees if possible
  • • Redeem points for high-value flights, not gadgets
  • • Review perks once a year to ensure the fee is still worth it
